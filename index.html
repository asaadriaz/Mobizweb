About Us

Get to know us and our work

Mobiz develops customized, high-performing mobile solutions for our business customers, and provides managed systems administration services. Mobiz has established a strong and growing reputation for providing differentiated mobile apps and desktop software that are developed and delivered with the highest quality. We also perform physical network installations and provide remote administration and maintenance of network components. We connect and configure entire offices with CAT 5/6 or Fiber optic cables, configure Cisco equipment, and remotely install systems and applications.

Delivering High-performing Solutions for our clients

Our development team has been hand-picked due to their previous experience in developing mobile apps for Fortune 1000 organizations and award-winning mobile games. Their expertise has been honed through robust instruction in mobile development and project management best practices.

Our managed systems team has worked on dozens of projects for CIOs and IT departments, and is best recognized by customers for their technical knowledge, planning expertise, and successful execution in fulfilling clients' unique requirements. To our clients, we are a talented, laser-focused, and agile mobile app development and managed IT systems provider that helps them connect their people, customers, processes, and technology investments.

Services

Mobile Apps and Managed Services

Mobile App Developer ~ Mobiz develops advanced and customized mobile apps, web- based solutions, and desktop software for our business clients. We have developed applications on all major mobile and desktop OS platforms: iOS (iPhone and iPad), Android, and BlackBerry on mobile and Windows and Mac on desktop.
Managed IT Services provider (MSP) ~ Mobiz performs physical network installations and provides remote administration and maintenance of network components. We connect and configure entire offices with CAT 5/6 or Fiber optic cables, configure Cisco equipment, and remotely install systems and applications.

Software Development Expertise

We connect the mobile solutions we develop with our clients' internal APIs, back office software and servers, public APIs, and other internal and third party applications into an integrated mobile solution. We have experience integrating media, content from multiple sources, streaming data, and complete social network feature sets within mobile solutions.

We develop unique algorithms to ensure photos, videos, and other large content sources populate inside your mobile solutions so users don't have to delay their tasks. Rather than wait for users to request additional content to load, we ensure that this content is being loaded before users need it, so it's ready for them when they're ready for it.

We apply the same mechanics, interactivity, and fluidity that go into the development of mobile games to our clients' mobile solutions. Users have far less patience with speed and performance issues on mobile than any other platform. From fly-out menus to interactive timelines, our mobile solutions' interactions work intuitively to meet ever increasing mobile user experience expectations.

MSP Services

Mobiz contracts with small and medium businesses and large enterprises to deliver network- based services, applications, and equipment to them, including fully- outsourced network management arrangements, IP telephony, messaging and call center management, virtual private networks (VPNs), managed firewalls, and monitoring, reporting, and troubleshooting of network servers.

Voice and data communications have historically been managed by our clients separately, but more and more we are combining these into a consolidated VOIP solution for them. Mobiz specializes in designing and performing RF spectrum analysis, and providing Wi-Fi consulting for wireless VOIP and Voice-over-Wireless LAN (VoWLAN), wireless multimedia, and mobile communication systems.

Mobiz specializes in creating the architecture and engineering designs for our clients' Directory Services, including Active Directory, PKI, DNS, WINS, DHCP, and LDAP services. We also monitor and lead complex troubleshooting for these systems that typically involve multiple technologies and applications, put in place simplified workflows and processes, and identify and strategize disaster recovery scenarios.
